WELCOME TO HEALTH CENTER, IIT KANPUR!

The Health Centre is committed to promoting the health and wellness of the campus community by providing high-quality prevention, education, and treatment services. The medical, counseling, and health promotion services are designed to help you stay healthy or get better so that you can minimize disruptions caused by injury and illness. The Health Centre aims to enhance the healthcare experience of the IITK campus community by providing healthcare with respect, consideration and confidentiality. Please review this website or reach out to the Heath Centre staff for details of healthcare resources, prevention methods, health education and general healthcare guidance.
